# Incorrect Items Policy

An incorrect item is one that differs from what the customer ordered (wrong product,
variant, or quantity).

- Reports are covered within **30 days of delivery**.
- Where the mismatch can be confirmed against the order, the remedy is a replacement of
  the correct item or a refund of the affected line.
- The customer is not required to pay return postage for an item sent in error.
- If the mismatch cannot be verified from the order and the customer's description, the
  case is escalated rather than resolved automatically.

Replacement dispatch or refund requires support approval.
