# Refunds Policy

Refunds are issued once a returned item is received and inspected, or when an item is
confirmed damaged, incorrect, or lost in transit.

- Refunds are made to the original payment method and never exceed the amount paid for
  the affected items.
- The total refunded against an order can never exceed the order's total paid amount.
- Refunds of any value require support-team approval before they are issued.
- Higher-value refunds require supervisor approval and a recorded reason.
- Refunds above **£250** are handled by the finance team outside the standard support
  flow and are not issued automatically.

Delivery charges are refunded only where the whole order is being returned due to a
Meridian & Co. error.
