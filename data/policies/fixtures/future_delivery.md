# Future Delivery Standards

These delivery standards take effect on 1 January 2027 and are not yet in force.

- Standard UK delivery will be provided within two working days.
- Next-day delivery will be offered on eligible orders placed before 3pm.

Until the effective date, the current delivery-delays policy applies.
