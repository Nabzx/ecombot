# Missing Deliveries Policy

A delivery is "missing" when the carrier has not marked it delivered and it is
significantly overdue, or when the customer reports non-receipt.

- If the carrier shows the parcel as **delivered** but the customer says it did not
  arrive, the case is escalated to a supervisor for investigation before any remedy.
  No automatic refund or replacement is issued in this situation.
- If the carrier shows the parcel as **lost** or in a failed/exception state, the
  customer is eligible for a re-delivery or refund, subject to approval.
- Investigations may include a carrier enquiry and confirmation of the delivery
  address.

This policy exists to protect against both genuine loss and fraudulent claims.
