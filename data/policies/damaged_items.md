# Damaged Items Policy

Items that arrive damaged are covered when reported within **30 days of delivery**.

- The customer may be asked to describe or provide a photo of the damage.
- Eligible remedies are a like-for-like replacement or a refund of the affected items.
- Damaged items do not need to be returned in their original packaging.
- Any replacement dispatch or refund requires support approval before it is actioned.

Reports of damage made after the 30-day window are handled case by case and are
escalated rather than resolved automatically.
