# Returns Policy

Meridian & Co. accepts returns of most items within **30 days of delivery**.

- Items must be unused and in a resaleable condition, with original packaging where
  practical. Damaged or incorrect items are exempt from the packaging requirement.
- The 30-day window is counted from the confirmed delivery date of the order.
- Once a return is received and inspected, an eligible refund is issued to the
  original payment method.
- Certain hygiene-sensitive items (for example opened bedding) may only be returned if
  faulty.

Returns requested after the 30-day window are not eligible unless the item is faulty,
damaged on arrival, or was sent in error.
