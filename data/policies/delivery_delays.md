# Delivery Delays Policy

Standard UK delivery is expected within the promised delivery date shown at checkout.

- If the promised date has passed by **3 days or fewer**, the order is treated as
  in-progress and the customer is reassured with current tracking.
- Delays of **4 to 9 days** past the promised date warrant an apology and, where
  appropriate, a goodwill option such as re-delivery.
- Delays of **10 days or more** past the promised date are escalated for investigation
  as a potential lost parcel.

Goodwill remedies that move money or dispatch a replacement require support approval.
